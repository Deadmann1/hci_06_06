3 User Profiles

{Describe the kinds of user the site is trying to attract.}

The site is specified to deliver inforamtion to tourists of Graz. But can also be useful for residents of the City.

{Group these users into categories according to their characteristics.}

  1. Tourists:


    1. Tourists want to get interesting spots in the city. If they already have a vague plan in their head what they want to do in Graz, they want to get specific information for that task. A good example would be that they want to visit Schlossberg. They know they want to visit Schlossbrer, but don't know exactly where it is and how to get onto it. Besides, they might be interested what they can do at Schlossberg, what Restaurants are there and how long should they plan for this trip, and what they might need to bring. This was described for Schlossberg, but can be applied to any other activity.
    2. In addion they might be looking for interesting activitys in the City. What is in theyr vacinity and how much it costs.
    3. Toursits are not just looking for fun activities just as hiking up the Schlossberg. They might want to go Shopping. They want maybe a specific shop just as Spar, but maybe also want to see what Clothing Shops Graz has to offer and where they are.

  2. Possible Toursits/Future Tourists:

    Possible Toursits are persons that might go to Graz but are not sure if this is the right city to visit for them.
    Future Toursits are persons that have decided that they want to go to Graz but they might still need inforamtion.
    1. They might look for the classic Culture or activities that are possible in Graz. Are the Music Festival, Museums etc. They want to go onto the city and see quickly in a few clicks which activities might interest them.
    2. Good hotels are often hard to find. They want to quickly get a some good recommendation. Maybe even in theyr target area.

  3. Residents

    Altough the site is mainly targeted at Toursits. Residents can still find use out of this site.
    1. Finding new things to do. People living long and short in Graz might be interested in trying out new things or seeing what theyr city actually has to offer. They want to see a lot of stuff to do maybe in categories in only a few clicks.
    2. Just like 3.1.c Residents might look for good shopping possibilities in Graz.
    3. Just like in 3.2.b Residents might want to look for good Hotels for theyr friends or families that might want to visit Graz.
